Adapted from [ui-ux-pro-max](https://github.com/nextlevelbuilder/ui-ux-pro-max-skill) — top 20 most versatile pairings.

## Usage Tips

- **Control panel / admin**: Use #12 (Dashboard Pro) or #6 (Modern Professional)
- **Landing page**: Use #7 (Tech Startup) or #8 (Clean Minimal) for modern feel
- **E-commerce**: Use #1 (Classic Elegant) for luxury, #6 for general
- **Documentation**: Use #16 (Developer First) or #2 (Editorial Modern)
- **Never use the same pairing across every project** — variety is intentional
- **Load only weights you use** — typically 400, 500, 600, 700 max
